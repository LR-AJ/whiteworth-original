A Guide on Business Trade License in Dubai


The Department of Economic Development (DED) in Dubai is in charge of issuing business licences to organisations. Based on their commercial activity, the DED has divided each company into various licence categories. Getting a business licence in Dubai gives you the ability to regulate your company and gives you access to a number of benefits.

What are the different forms of Business Licenses available in Dubai?

Businesses must acquire a specific licence in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, depending on the nature of their operations. Business licences are granted by the Department of Economic Development (DED), which has divided companies into different categories. In Dubai, some of the most popular forms of business licences are:

  • Commercial License Businesses engaged in normal trading activities, such as the import and export of goods, must have a commercial licence
  • Professional licence Companies offering expert services like consulting, accounting, legal advice, and other corresponding services must have a Professional License.
  • Industrial Licence To conduct business in Dubai, you must have an industrial licence if your company engages in manufacturing, production, or industrial activities
  • Tourism License Obtaining a tourism licence is necessary for Companies involved in the travel and tourism sector, including tour operators, travel agencies, and hotels, are required to acquire a tourism licence from the DED

How can you apply for a Trading License?

It is necessary to take into consideration; the sort of business being conducted while applying for a trade licence in Dubai. A general trading licence is sufficient for companies that deal with import-export, apparel, accessories, furniture, and electronics. However, a commercial trade licence is necessary for enterprises engaged in activities like selling automobiles, firearms, alcohol, and medical supplies and must have approval from higher authorities or UAE municipalities.

A commercial trade licence in the Mainland of Dubai involves more paperwork and clearance from the appropriate authorities, therefore the application process is more demanding. It is significant to remember that anyone or any company seeking to engage in business activities in Dubai must first obtain a trading licence.

process to renew a Trade Licence in UAE


For firms in Dubai, renewing a trade licence is a critical procedure. The following actions must be taken to ensure an effortless renewal:

Authentic Tenancy Contract

Verifying that the company has a legitimate tenancy contract for the office space is the first step. A minimum of one month must pass after the application date before the tenancy agreement expires.


Application for Licence Renewal

Applying for a licence renewal is the next step. This is possible through the official service centres or the website of the Dubai Department of Economic Development (DED)


Payment

Following the application's submission, the renewal payments are due. The cost varies according to the type of business and the license's period of validity.


Free Consultation

To assist with the renewal procedure, the DED offers free consultation services to businesses. Businesses must renew their trade licence before it expires. Penalties and fines may apply if this is not done. Businesses can guarantee a hassle-free renewal of their trade licence in Dubai by taking these measures.
