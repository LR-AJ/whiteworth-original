How to Acquire a Tourism License in Dubai


One of the world's most robust and consistently expanding commercial sectors is found in Dubai, along with other emirates. Despite occasionally occurring difficulties, the business sector in Dubai is rarely affected. Dubai's commercial environment presents one of the strongest options for wealth expansion for investors looking for new investment alternatives. Due to its great value and chances for new investors, the city is a popular choice for investors from all over the world. Additionally, Dubai draws millions of tourists each year and is a well-known tourist destination worldwide.

Dubai offers several strong arguments for investing in the travel and tourism sector. The city is a top choice for tourism investors because of its innovative architecture, inviting beaches, agreeable temperature, and constantly developing vacation culture. When considering to establish a tourist business in Dubai, where the sector has become evergreen and generates significant returns for investors, these considerations are an essential element of the decision-making process

A tourist business must be registered with the Department of Economic Development and the Department of Dubai tourist and Commerce Marketing in order to be established in Dubai. When applying for one of the numerous tourism business licences that the Dubai government offers, investors must be well-informed. The issue of travel and tourist licences is overseen by the Department of tourist and Commerce Marketing (DTCM). As a result, business owners wishing to launch a travel and tourism company in Dubai must get in touch with the same organisation. The travel and tourism sector provides three types of licenses based on the services offered.

You can apply for one of the following licences provided by the Department of tourist and Commerce Marketing (DTCM) if you wish to launch a travel and tourist company in Dubai:

  • Inbound tour operator License

    • A company can manage travel arrangements within Dubai, such as event participation, visa services, and transportation services for tourists, with the help of an inbound tour operator licence.

  • Outbound tourist license

    • A corporation can offer vacation packages outside of the country with the help of internet channels or by recruiting offline agents with an outbound tourist licence.

  • Travel agent license

    • This licence enables a business to act as a vendor and offer customers ticketing and booking services, including for travel on planes, trains, buses, hotels and other related services.

    The Tourism License Fee

    The DTCM Authority unveiled a new rule in October 2018 to make it simpler for investors to acquire a tourism licence in Dubai. The rule reduced the initial investment and costs necessary by doing away with the requirement for a bank guarantee of AED 100,000–AED 200,000 for each activity, such as an incoming tour operator, an outgoing tour operator, and a travel agency

    It is now simpler for new investors to submit an application for a tourism licence in Dubai owing to the removal of the minimum square footage requirement of 300 feet as well as the elimination of requirements for documents such as experience letters and educational certificates for managers in the same sector.

    Documents for Dubai Tourism License

    To obtain a tourism license in Dubai, an applicant must provide the following documents

    • Completed application form
    • Copy of applicant's passport
    • Proof of educational and professional qualifications of the manager
    • Certificate of no criminal record for both the company owner and manager
    • Certificate of no objection (NOC) issued by the Civil Aviation Authority.

    Setup for valid tourism license

    The following business establishments require a valid tourism license to operate in Dubai and UAE

    Travel firms
    Hotels
    Restaurants
    Car rental firms
    Travel agents

    The owner can carry out a number of duties when the establishment of a tourism business in Dubai is complete and the licence is granted, including:

    • Issuing travel insurance to customers
    • Offering vacation packages via both offline and internet platforms
    • Providing tourists with services connected to visas
    • Providing customers with a variety of lodging and stay options
    • Selling tickets for flights, trains, and buses to tourists
    • Providing tourists with automobile rental services
    • Selling a range of adventure packages

    Benefits of Tourism License

    Licences for tourism-related businesses have consistently been quite popular in the UAE, particularly in Dubai.

    • In Dubai, a travel agent licence, an outgoing tour operator licence, and an incoming tour operator licence can all be combined under a single licence without any extra charges or bank assurances
    • The upfront costs of starting a tourism business in Dubai can be greatly reduced by obtaining a tourism licence
    • With a tourism licence, the business is able to conduct operations within and outside of the local tourism market, as well as plan events like shows, exhibits, and international conferences.
    • Subject to immigration approval, the business can sell plane tickets and visitor visas in the UAE with a travel agent licence issued under the tourism licence
    • The business is able to provide lodging, organise guided or planned tours, and arrange transportation for customers given that it holds a travel agent and inbound tourist licence under the tourism licence
    • The rules and regulations of the government in Dubai, one of the world's top tourist destinations renowned for its top-notch infrastructure and tourism attractions, are advantageous to investors in a licence for the travel and tourism industry.
