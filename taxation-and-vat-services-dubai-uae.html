Vat Registration

A crucial prerequisite for companies operating in the UAE is VAT registration. For businesses with an annual turnover of AED 375,000 or more, it is mandatory whereas for businesses with an annual turnover of AED 187,500 to AED 375,000, it is not mandatory. Businesses can still opt to register for VAT in such circumstances in order to profit from the tax advantages and raise their market reputation.
